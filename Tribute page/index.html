BTS Leader RM

Dedicated to the life and achievements of Kim Namjoon

Biography

Kim Nam-joon (Korean: 김남준; born September 12, 1994), known professionally as RM (formerly Rap Monster), is a South Korean rapper, songwriter and record producer. He is the leader of South Korean boy band BTS.

RM selected the name "Rap Monster" during his time as an idol trainee. But he formally changed his stage name to "RM" in November 2017 during BTS' DNA era, as he felt that "Rap Monster" was no longer representative of who he was as a person.

Career

RM began his career as a rapper in middle school when he grew interested in lyricism. In 2007, as a first year middle school student, RM began rapping in local amateur hip-hop circles and then the underground k-hip-hop scene under the pseudonym 'Runch Rancha.'

RM auditioned and joined BigHit Entertainment at the age of 16. A few years later, on August 20th, 2014, he went on to debut in BTS with their first studio album 'Dark and Wild.' In 2015, RM released his first solo-mixtape. Then in 2018, he released his most recent and second mixtape, 'Mono.' Since their debut, the group has released 9 studio albums together.

As a student, RM had an IQ of 148 and scored in the top 1% of the nation in the university entrance examinations for language, math, foreign language and social studies.Because his parents had been strongly opposed to his interest in a musical career due to his academic achievements, RM initially decided to set music aside to focus on his studies. He eventually convinced his mother to allow him to be a rapper, asking her if "she wanted to have a son who was a first-place rapper, or a 5,000th-place student".

In March 2019, after graduating from Global Cyber University with a degree in Broadcasting and Entertainment, RM enrolled at Hanyang Cyber University's Master of Business Administration program in Advertising and Media.

Achievements

  • RM occupied the top 3 spots on Billboard’s World Digital Song Sales chart for the week ending on June 8! “Nuts” debuted at no. 1 on the chart, followed by “Groin” at no. 2 and “Domodachi” at no. 3. This marks RM’s fifth no. 1 and 25th entry overall on the chart. Furthermore, RM is now the second South Korean soloist to occupy the top 3 spots simultaneously on the World Digital Song Sales chart multiple times.
  • RM’s "Right Place, Wrong Person" debuted at the top of Billboard’s Top Rap Albums chart (dated June 8), earning him his first no. 1 on the chart! RM joins SUGA as the only two South Korean artists to ever reach no. 1 on the chart. Furthermore, six of the songs from his album debuted on Billboard’s Rap Digital Song Sales chart, which ranks the top 10 most popular rap songs for the week.
  • The official music video for RM’s “Wild Flower (with youjeen)” surpassed 100 million views on YouTube! Released on December 2, 2022, it took the music video approximately one year, four months and 25 days to hit this milestone. It is RM’s second solo music video following “Persona” to reach reach 100 million views.
  • On September 27, it was revealed that RM recently made a 100 million won donation to the Korean Forensic Society. The donation was made through forensic scientist and professor at Chonbuk National University, Lee Ho, whom RM met through the tVN variety show, ‘The Dictionary of Useless Human Knowledge’. The donation will be used towards academic research, talent training, and forensic system improvement projects.
  • In a ceremony that took place on June 1 (KST), RM was officially appointed as an ambassador for South Korea’s Ministry of National Defense’s (MND) Agency for Killed in Action (KIA) Recovery & Identification (MAKRI). RM is the third ever to receive this honor. The organization was launched in 2000 to recover and identify the remains of fallen Korean War veterans and return them to their families. As an ambassador, RM will participate in various public relations activities to promote the agency and inform the public of its work.
  • On May 31, 2022, along with other member of BTS, RM visited US President Joe Biden at the White House to discuss the recent rise in anti-Asian hate crimes and discrimination.
  • On March 30, RM was announced as the new brand ambassador for the luxury fashion label, Bottega Veneta. He becomes the first and only celebrity brand ambassador for the brand label. RM recently attended their Autumn/Winter fashion show in Milan.
  • On December 21, the Arts Council Korea named RM as one of the ten '2020 Art Patrons of the Year.' The award recognizes those who have helped promote and support the local arts scene. RM was recognized for his support for the arts through his 100 million won donation to the National Museum of Modern and Contemporary Art in September. The donation was used to produce out-of-print art books and distribute them to 400 libraries and schools across the country.
